Our Story

At Hive, companies seek out talented students. We help students from diverse backgrounds become tech-savvy professionals, whether that's through exposure to cutting-edge technology, inspiring teams, or competitive salaries. Today, Hive is Africa's leading job network focused on student developers, serving regions across Africa, America, and Europe.
